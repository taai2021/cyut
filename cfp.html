Call for Papers

TAAI 2021 consists of both the international track and domestic track. Papers on academic researches and industrial applications of artificial intelligence and related areas are welcome. TAAI 2021 is open to international participants. We also welcome experienced scholars to organize special sessions to detail specific topics.


Topics of Interest

The conference will cover a wide range of AI research topics and applications, including but not limited to:

  • Autonomous Agents and Distributed AI
  • Computer Games
  • Computer Vision / Pattern Recognition
  • Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery
  • Federated Learning
  • Fuzzy Systems and Fuzzy Neural Networks
  • Genetic and Evolutionary Computation
  • Knowledge Representation and Automatic Reasoning
  • Knowledge-Based Systems
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Problem Solving and Search
  • Recommender Systems
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Speech Recognition and Synthesis
  • Web Intelligence and Social Networks


Submission

International track: Papers for the international track have to be written and presented in English. Manuscripts should be submitted in PDF format and formatted using the IEEE 2-column format, including the bibliography within 6 pages. Submissions to TAAI 2021 are single-blind, i.e. the names affiliations of the authors should be listed in the submitted version. Selected papers from the international track will be invited to submit an extended version for a special issue in the Journal of Information Science and Engineering after the conference.

Important information:

  • At least one author per accepted paper must register and attend the conference to present for the accepted submission to be included in the conference proceedings.
    • 　
  • Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore.

Instructions regarding camera ready and registration:

  • Authors of accepted papers should submit the camera-ready copy before Sep. 30, 2021. We will send further information on the copyright form requested by the Conference Publishing once the copyright form is ready.
    • 　
  • Papers that do not have a full-registered author by the early-bird registration deadline (Sep. 30, 2021) will be removed from the proceedings. In the case of multiple acceptances, each extra paper requires at least one extra Full Registration. Please register via our onine registration system

Important deadlines are:

  • Paper Submission Deadline: July 31, 2021
  • Notification of paper acceptance: September 21, 2021
  • Camera-ready and Registration: September 30, 2021
  • Conference Dates: November 18~20, 2021
