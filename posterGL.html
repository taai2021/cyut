Oral presentation instructions


  • Each presentation is 15 minutes long. It is recommended to use 12 minutes to present and 3 minutes for discussion/questions.
  • The available equipment will be a Laptop, a projector, a laser pointer and microphones. The Laptop will have sound to facilitate the use of DVD clips or videos.
  • PThe session Laptops will have access to the Internet.
  • The recommended file type to be used for presentations is PowerPoint or Portable Document Format (PDF).
  • The presentation files should be brought on a USB, memory stick or a similar memory device.
  • Speakers are requested to upload their presentation on to the session Laptop and report to the session chair 15 minutes before the start of the session.
  • All presenters will be introduced to the audience by the session chair.


Poster presentation instructions


  • All authors must print and bring their posters with them to the conference.
  • You can download the Poster template here. ( Poster template; Zip file download )
  • Poster format: 60 cm X 90 cm, straight.The poster text should be written from left to right, and the font size should be visible at a distance of one meter.
  • The content of the poster should include
    • the title of the paper
    • the name(s) of the authors(s)
    • the name of the institution
    • the purpose of the research
    • the method
    • the discussion of the results
    • the conclusion
  • Onsite Information
    • Push pins or Velcro will be provided to attach your posters to the boards.
    • Your poster must be removed immediately after the poster session. If your poster is left behind, it will be discarded.


國內議程之海報展示指引


  • 所有作者必須列印並攜帶海報參加會議。
  • 您可以在此處下載海報模板。 ( 海報模板 ; Zip file download )
  • 海報格式：60 cm X 90 cm，直式版面。海報文字應從左到右書寫，字體大小在一米處可以清晰閱讀。
  • 海報內容應包括：
    • 論文的標題
    • 作者姓名
    • 機構名稱
    • 研究的目的
    • 方法
    • 結果的討論
    • 結論
  • 現場將提供圖釘或魔術貼將您的海報固定在板上。
  • 您的海報必須在海報展示後立即移除。

Virtual presentation instructions


  • Presentations must be delivered in English.
  • Each presentation is 15 minutes long. It is recommended to use 12 minutes to present and 3 minutes for discussion/questions.
  • PVirtual presentations are via Google Meet, while the corresponding web links are listed within Conference Program for each session. Each virtual session is with two links, i.e., link 1 is the major one and link 2 for backup purpose.
  • As there is another Laptop to broadcast and record all virtual presentations, speakers are requested to report to the session chair 15 minutes before the start of the session.
  • The other recommendations and guidelines for virtual presentations can be found within the section of the Oral presentation instructions.
