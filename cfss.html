Call for Special Session

The special sessions welcome the interesting authors to contribute the papers. The organizer of each special session will be invited as the session chair, and he/she should take all responsibility to carry out review process. All the special sessions will be centralized as tracks in the same conference submission and reviewing system. The format of each submission is the same as the regular paper. The related information about paper submission is described as follows.


Paper Types

  • All papers should be submitted electronically in PDF version of your paper through the Paper Submission System whose hyperlinks (URLs) are listed in the following table.
  • The special session accepts two types of submissions for review. These types of papers need to be submitted in IEEE two-column format and should be written in English. And they will be included in the conference proceeding.
  • Manuscript should be unpublished work, must include enough detail about the goal, the problem to solve, proposed methods and main results.
  • The length of the final camera-ready paper is 4-6 pages. Papers exceeding 6 pages may be submitted upon payment of overlength page charges. A maximum of 2 extra pages above 6 pages are permitted. For papers of more than 6 pages, the page charge is US$40 (NT$1,200) per additional page.
  • In order to provide more incentives for paper submissions, authors can have an option to select whether they want their papers to be included in IEEE Xplore Digital Library.


Special Session Submission

International track: Papers for the international track have to be written and presented in English. Manuscripts should be submitted in PDF format and formatted using the IEEE 2-column format, including the bibliography within 6 pages. Submissions to TAAI 2021 are single-blind, i.e. the names affiliations of the authors should be listed in the submitted version.

Important information:

  • IEEE Manuscript Templates： Download
  • At least one author per accepted paper must register and attend the conference to present for the accepted submission to be included in the conference proceedings.
    • 　
  • Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore.

Instructions regarding camera ready and registration:

  • Authors of accepted papers should submit the camera-ready copy before Sep. 30, 2021. We will send further information on the copyright form requested by the Conference Publishing once the copyright form is ready.
    • 　
  • Papers that do not have a full-registered author by the early-bird registration deadline (Sep. 30, 2021) will be removed from the proceedings. In the case of multiple acceptances, each extra paper requires at least one extra Full Registration. Please register via our online registration system.
  • If you have travel restrictions due to COVID-19, you may still publish your paper at TAAI 2021 conference with discounted fee.

Important deadlines are:

  • Paper Submission Deadline: July 31, 2021
  • Notification of paper acceptance: September 21, 2021
  • Camera-ready and Registration: September 30, 2021
  • Conference Dates: November 18~20, 2021
